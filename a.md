# Concept 1: Bias-Variance Tradeoff

error from overly simplistic assumptions. 
High bias → model underfits, misses patterns even in training data.

Variance = error from sensitivity to fluctuations in training data. High variance → model overfits, memorizes noise, fails to generalize.

The tradeoff: as you increase model complexity, bias goes down but variance goes up. The goal is the sweet spot that minimizes total error on unseen data.
